The Mind and Our Choices






Mock Blog 1: Why We Do What We Do

Written By: Jimmy Diaz on April 1, 2023


I asked myself one day: Why do we do what we do? Why do people choose to climb Mount Everest? Why do people choose to jump out of planes? Why do people choose to use violence when things are not going their way? I pondered upon this question, and throughout my profound thoughts, I realized human beings are intricate specimens. We have the ability to contemplate abstract topics due to our superior mental processing. Although we are all humans, we are all unique from each other, and this individuality drives us to do what we do. Below, I have included a video about the human brain and an interactive graph that compares number of nerve cells in the brains of certain species.

In Summary:

    • The next closest animal has about 10 trillion less nerve cells in its cerebral cortex
  3. We are the most intelligent species
